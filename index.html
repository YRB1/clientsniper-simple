Our Story
Two Mates Who Got Tired of the Old Way

We're Ben and Finn. We love sales — always have. There's nothing like that buzz when you close a deal, land a client, or watch a cold lead turn warm. But after years of grinding, we kept hitting the same wall: the tools were slow, the data was dodgy, and the "industry standard" methods felt like they were designed in 2012.

Buying lead lists that were 60% dead emails. Copy-pasting from Google Maps one business at a time. Paying per lead like it's some kind of luxury. We looked at each other and thought — this is mental. There has to be a better way for people who actually have the passion and drive but don't want to waste half their day on busywork.

So we built ClientSniper. Not as some faceless SaaS — but as the exact tool we wished existed when we were starting out. Fast, honest, and built for people like us: hustlers, freelancers, small agencies — anyone who'd rather spend their time closing deals than hunting for email addresses. We're not trying to be the biggest. We're trying to be the one you actually enjoy using.

FAQ
Common Questions
How does the 30-day free trial work?+

Sign up for any plan and get full access for 30 days. We won't charge your card until day 31. Cancel before then and you pay nothing.

Where does ClientSniper get its data?+

ClientSniper analyses publicly available data — business listings, websites, and social profiles that are already visible online. We comply with GDPR and data protection regulations. You're responsible for how you use the data you collect.

How many businesses can I search?+

Every plan includes unlimited searches. You can analyse up to 1,000 businesses per search and run as many searches as you like across any city and niche combination.

Can I export data to my CRM?+

Yes — export to CSV, Excel, or push directly to HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive and more. The Bundle plan also includes Zapier integration for 5,000+ apps.

How does the outreach template generator work?+

Select any lead and click "Generate Outreach". ClientSniper automatically writes a personalised cold email or DM using the business name, city, niche, and the specific website issue detected (e.g. no website, poor mobile, outdated design). You review it and send — no copy-pasting required.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Absolutely. Cancel from your dashboard in one click. No contracts, no lock-ins, no cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer team plans?+

Yes — you can add additional team members to your workspace at £4/month per seat. Each seat gets full access to all tools in your plan. Contact us to add seats or for custom agency licensing.
