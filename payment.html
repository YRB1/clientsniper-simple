FAQ
Pricing Questions
How does the 30-day free trial work?+

Sign up for any plan and get full, unrestricted access for 30 days. We won't charge your card until day 31. Cancel before then and you pay nothing — no tricks, no small print.

Can I switch plans later?+

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the end of your billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. All payments are processed securely — we never store your card details.

Is there a contract or lock-in?+

No contracts, no lock-ins. Cancel from your dashboard in one click at any time. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer refunds?+

Because we offer a 30-day free trial on every plan, we don't provide refunds after the trial period. You have a full month to test everything risk-free before committing.

Can I use ClientSniper for my clients?+

Yes! Many agencies use ClientSniper to generate leads for their clients. Each plan is tied to your account — scrape as much as you need and export leads for any number of clients.

What's the difference between Starter and Outreach plans?+

The Starter plan gives you lead discovery, website analysis, opportunity scoring, and smart filters — everything you need to find the right businesses to target. The Outreach plan adds AI-generated outreach templates, email campaigns, phone click-to-call, and follow-up tracking so you can contact those leads and close them. The Agency plan adds auto sequences, team seats, and CRM integrations.
